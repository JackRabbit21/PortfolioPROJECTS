A 12-slide strategy deck covering:
1. Title

5. Where AI Cuts Costs (deep dive)
6. Where AI Creates Revenue (deep dive)
7. Prioritization Matrix (Impact vs. Speed to Value)
8. What to Implement First — Year 1

10. Estimated Value at Stake

Full written narrative — good for sharing via email or as a brief. Covers all the same frameworks as the deck but in prose form with tables.

### data/use_case_matrix.csv
14 AI use cases scored by category, impact, speed to value, investment range, and expected return. Import into Excel or Sheets to sort/filter.
